First of all, you should know that Spotify has three different kinds of quality in its streaming player, namely: Normal Quality, High Quality, and Extreme Quality (the latter only for Premium subscribers).

However, you have to consider that the quality of the music does not only depend on the player, but also on the equipment you are using. In normal quality, a mobile device receives 96 kbps. For desktop computers and Spotify web player, in normal quality, it supports 160kbps. In high quality, a mobile device allows audio at 160 kbps. For Web and Desktop Player in high quality, receive 320 kbps (only for Premium version). For the mobile version, Extreme Quality supports audio at 320 kbps. (Only for Premium subscribers).
